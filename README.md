🤖 Job Market Analytics Dashboard (2025–2026)



📌 Project Overview



This project analyzes the global AI job market using \*\*Excel, SQL, and Power BI\*\*. The dashboard provides insights into salary trends, job demand, company size, experience levels, remote work, and industry hiring patterns. It helps identify high-paying AI roles and understand current hiring trends through interactive visualizations.



\---



🎯 Objectives



\- Analyze AI job market trends.

\- Compare salaries across countries and experience levels.

\- Identify the highest-paying AI roles.

\- Analyze job openings by industry.

\- Study remote, hybrid, and on-site work distribution.

\- Evaluate demand score and benefits score across industries.
